Our Story

We met in a world full of possibilities and found something special in each other. Every day since then has been an adventure we're grateful to share together.

What We Love

Hey there! We are Aljhon & Estella, a fun-loving couple who share a passion for video games, movies, and collecting cool stuff!

Most of our free time is spent playing games together, whether it's co-op adventures or competitive battles. We also love movie nights, exploring everything from action-packed blockbusters to nostalgic classics.

Aside from gaming and movies, we're both passionate collectors of Hot Wheels and Hirons, always on the hunt for rare and unique pieces to add to our collection!

This space is a little glimpse into our world, filled with our favorite memories, photos, and videos. Welcome, and enjoy!
